Esther B. Cho

Experienced Content and Media Marketer, based in Brooklyn New York.




Experience

Esther has over a decade of content and media advertising marketing experience working with Fortune 500 Brands. She's spent her career working with a vast category of brands at agencies like The Content Collective, OMD/Media Arts Lab, and Carat. In addition to, she spent a stint on the publisher side at a popular e-newsletter company - DailyCandy. Currently, she's volunteering at With Warm Welcome as Creative Director.

Content Strategy

Brainstorming, drafting briefs and legal agreements, planning in tandem with media, project managing campaigns, activation and driving results to over deliver and achieve brand KPI's.

Influencer Activation

Identifying and vetting authentic talent who have an affinity for the brand, recruiting talent and negotiating contracts inclusive of brand content usage rights, and managing influencer campaigns.

Digital Media Planning

Developing and maintaining cross channel media campaigns with strategic partners, negotiating power to ensure brand receives the most cost effective rates and value.







Services

Strategy

Developing data informed strategy for content, digital & traditional media, influencer, and experiential activations while aligning back to brand KPI's and objectives.

Planning

Cohesive cross-channel planning and project management of general market and multicultural campaigns. Working in lock step with clients, integrated agency teams, and partners.

Writing

Copywriting for content and media brand campaign assets as well as editorial content creation and copyediting for blogs, social posts, and websites.

Results

Analyzing and measurement of content and media campaigns. Creating wrap up reports to showcase success and identifying optimizations to help inform future campaigns.
